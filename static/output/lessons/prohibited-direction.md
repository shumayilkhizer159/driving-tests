## No entry for drivers in both directions

### Prohibitive traffic sign

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_3.jpg) | This prohibitive sign indicates that no drivers (including cyclists) are allowed **to drive in both directions past this sign**.  Oncoming traffic is impossible. |

### Plate

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_4.jpg) | A plate can indicate when **cyclists and drivers of a moped class A** are allowed to drive past this sign. |

### Except local traffic

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_5.jpg) | Sometimes you can see a **blue plate** with the text: 'Uitgezonderd plaatselijk verkeer'.  Who is allowed to drive past the sign:   * the vehicles of the residents of that street and of the visitors of these residents. * the vehicles for delivering goods or maintenance or supervision. * the emergency vehicles and priority vehicles. * and cyclists and moped riders. |

## No entry for vehicles

### Prohibitive traffic sign 1

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_6.jpg) | ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_7.png)  This prohibitive sign indicates **no entry in this direction** past this sign to drivers of all vehicles:   * having more than two wheels. * and motorcycles with a side car.   Oncoming traffic is allowed. |

### Prohibitive traffic sign 2

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_8.jpg) | ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_9.png)  This prohibitive sign indicates **no entry in this direction** past this sign for drivers of:   * **motorcycles,** * **and motorcycles with a side car.**   However, they are allowed to drive in the opposite direction. A driver of a car is allowed to drive past this sign. |

### Prohibitive traffic sign 3

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_10.jpg) | ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_11.png)  A combination of these two signs is also possible.  It prohibits to drive past this sign:   * **drivers of motor vehicles with more than two wheels,** * **and all motorcycles.**   However, they are allowed to drive in the opposite direction. |

### Prohibitive traffic sign 4

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_12.jpg) | ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_13.png)  No entry for quad bike drivers = four-wheel motor vehicles, constructed for unpaved terrain, with an open body, a motorcycle-like handlebar and a saddle. |

## Maximum gross weight

### M.G.W.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_14.jpg) | In lesson 9 we read that the M.G.W. is the weight of a car with the driver and the passengers and its load when one puts this on a scale **at a particular moment**. |

### Prohibitive traffic sign

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| ![](images/prohibited-direction_img_15.jpg) | This traffic sign indicates no entry to vehicles exceeding the maximum gross weight.  With your car (3,5 ton) you may drive past this sign. |
